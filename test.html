Speed & Velocity

  • The measurement of speed in a certain direction of motion.
  • The speed is an absolute value of the velocity, meaning that a speed must always be positive, but the velocity can be negative - against the direction of the axis.
  • For each section the velocity is the same, but between sections the velocity is different.

Formulas

  • v = (Δx/Δt) - to calculate the velocity
  • x = x0 + v * Δt - calculate the position at moment t
    • x = the position at t
    • x0 = the starting position
    • v = velocity
    • t = time
